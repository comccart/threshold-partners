

# Threshold Partners — Professional Consulting Website

A polished, single-page consulting website with a confident, strategic feel using the specified brand palette and typography.

---

## 1. Sticky Header
- "THRESHOLD PARTNERS" logo text in navy, Montserrat Bold
- Two nav buttons: "The Leadership Gap" and "Book a Call" in orange/gold
- Cream background, smooth scroll navigation

## 2. Hero Section
- Full-width navy background with cream text
- Bold headline: "Cross the AI adoption threshold with clarity and confidence"
- Supporting subheadline describing the value proposition
- Two orange CTA buttons: "Download: The Leadership Gap" and "Book a Call"

## 3. Problem / Solution Section
- Two-column layout on cream background
- Left: "The Challenge" with rust red accent — four pain points
- Right: "What You Need" with cerulean accent — four solutions
- Clean iconography for each bullet point

## 4. The Offer — Fractional AI Lead
- Centered section on white background
- "6-Month Engagement" subtitle
- Three phase cards on cream backgrounds:
  - Months 1–2: Diagnose & Design (rust red accent)
  - Months 3–4: Build & Implement (cerulean accent)
  - Months 5–6: Transfer & Scale (orange accent)
- Pricing: €45,000 (€7,500/month) with "Book a Call" CTA

## 5. Testimonials
- Three white cards on cream background with subtle cerulean borders
- Quotes from Marcus Mellberg, Waseem Iqbal, and David Bermingham
- Names/titles in rust red, Montserrat Medium

## 6. Lead Magnet — "The Leadership Gap"
- Centered section with book title and description
- Simple book cover mockup placeholder in navy
- "Download the Guide" orange CTA button

## 7. Footer
- Navy background with cream text
- Company name, tagline, email, LinkedIn and calendar links
- Copyright line

## Design & Technical Details
- Google Fonts: Montserrat + Lato loaded via HTML/CSS
- Fully mobile responsive with stacked layouts on small screens
- Smooth scroll navigation between sections
- Orange used sparingly — only on CTAs
- Generous whitespace, subtle shadows on cards, cerulean hover borders
- All built as a single React page with clean component structure
